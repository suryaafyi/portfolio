⚠️ 2. PROBLEM
“Memories don’t disappear. They just lose their place.”

Today, meaningful moments are scattered across apps:

Photos in galleries
Conversations in chats
Reminders in calendars

Nothing connects them into a single, living memory system.

Key Problems

1. Scattered across platforms
Memories live in silos — no unified timeline.

2. Built for sharing, not remembering
Platforms optimize engagement, not meaning.

3. No space designed for families
No private, structured environment for shared memories.

👉 Result:
Important moments become unstructured, unseen, or forgotten.

💡 3. SOLUTION
“Knot, a space designed to help families capture moments, stay connected, and remember what truly matters — together.”

A private, shared space where:

Memories
People
Moments

…come together in one place.

Core Pillars

Intentional Focus
Prioritizes intimacy over noise.

Seamless Archiving
Automatic organization of shared media.

Cross-Generational
Accessible for both grandparents and digital natives.
