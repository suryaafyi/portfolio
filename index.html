About ⌘

I'm a product designer( he/him ) with a background in Software Engineering. I love crafting a meaningful interactions and bringing fun ideas to life. Currently based in Chennai, India.

I recently completed a UI/UX design program at HCL GUVI, where I not only acquired technical expertise but also developed a profound appreciation for the beautiful union of aesthetics and functionality and got hands-on with the entire design process.

It wasn’t just about learning tools, but about learning how to think, how to break down a problem, question decisions, and design with intent. I don’t just think about how something looks, I think about how it works, how it scales, and how it will be implemented.

I navigate the exciting world of product design, where every pixel serves a purpose. Combining a deep understanding of user experience with a knack for transforming ideas into visually stunning interfaces, I approach each project with a burning passion to craft something truly remarkable.

I design to make people smile 😊
